About Elisaac Schools

A faith-rooted primary and secondary learning community committed to academic excellence, character formation, and joyful discovery.

Our Mission

Inspire lifelong learning while building strong spiritual, moral and academic foundations.

Our Values

Kindness, integrity, curiosity, resilience, service and faith guide every interaction.

Our Philosophy & Approach

We believe every child is uniquely gifted. Learning at Elisaac blends rigorous academics with creativity, service, and spiritual growth. Lessons emphasize problem solving, collaboration, and real‑world relevance so learners connect knowledge to purpose.

Holistic Development

Academic, social-emotional, physical and spiritual growth planned intentionally.

Active Learning

Projects, debates, labs and guided inquiry energize curiosity.

Faith & Character

Daily routines reinforce respect, service, gratitude and responsibility.

Community & Partnership
