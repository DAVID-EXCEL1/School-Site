Progression Map

Each stage builds readiness for the next, blending academic rigor with moral and spiritual guidance.

  1. Early Years: Discovery, play, phonics, number sense.
  2. Primary: Core mastery, reading fluency, problem solving.
  3. Junior Secondary: Exploratory breadth, analytical thinking, collaboration.
  4. Senior Secondary: Depth, specialization, leadership, exam readiness.
